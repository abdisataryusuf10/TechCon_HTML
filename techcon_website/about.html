Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in a community center. What started as a modest event with just 200 attendees has now grown into one of the premier technology conferences in the world, attracting over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing a platform for groundbreaking announcements, product launches, and thought-provoking discussions. Our conference has hosted some of the most influential figures in technology and has been the launchpad for numerous successful startups.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the global technology community. We believe that technology has the power to solve the world's most pressing challenges and improve lives across the globe.

"To create a inclusive platform where technology professionals, enthusiasts, and visionaries can connect, learn, and shape the future together."

We are committed to diversity, sustainability, and accessibility in technology. Each year, we strive to create an event that not only showcases cutting-edge technology but also addresses the ethical, social, and environmental implications of technological progress.

Past Notable Speakers

TechCon has been privileged to host some of the most influential and innovative minds in technology. Here are just a few of the remarkable speakers who have graced our stages in previous years:

Dr. Sarah Chen

AI Research Pioneer

Dr. Chen presented her groundbreaking work on ethical AI frameworks at TechCon 2022. Her research has shaped industry standards for responsible AI development.

Marcus Johnson

Quantum Computing Expert

At TechCon 2021, Marcus demonstrated the first practical application of quantum computing in cryptography, revolutionizing data security approaches.

Priya Sharma

Sustainable Tech Innovator

Priya's 2020 keynote on technology solutions for climate change inspired numerous startups focused on environmental sustainability.

These visionaries, along with many others, have made significant contributions to the tech industry and have shared their insights exclusively at TechCon. We continue this tradition in 2024 with an exceptional lineup of speakers who are shaping the future of technology.
